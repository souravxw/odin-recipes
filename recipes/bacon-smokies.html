Bacon Smokies

Description

These sweet and salty bacon-wrapped smokies are a quick and easy party appetizer.

Serve them straight from the oven or keep them warm in a slow cooker until the big game starts.

Assembling these smoky bacon wraps may take some time, but it's well worth it!

Ingradients

  • Bacon
  • Coctail winers
  • Toothpics
  • Brown sugar

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Line a large, rimmed baking sheet with parchment paper or aluminum foil.
  3. Evenly sprinkle brown sugar over the tops of the bacon-wrapped wieners.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until bacon is crisp and sugar is bubbly, about 40 minutes.
  5. Serve immediately, or place in a slow cooker set on Low to keep warm.
